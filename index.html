Important limitations

This is a prototype. It scores OSM tags, not official safety, accessibility, crash risk, grades, snow clearing, lighting, or real-time traffic. Missing OSM data can make places look worse than they really are.

Tip: zoom in to a neighbourhood before calculating. Click a coloured spot to see its score.
